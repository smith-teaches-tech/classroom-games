    3A better ruler

    Ask all four at once

    Single rules failed — each one crowned something absurd. So stop asking "is it science?" and ask these four questions of every card instead. This is the grid. It travels: later this year we'll point it at things that aren't science at all.

    The grid — four questions for any claim

    EEvidence. What counts as evidence here — and could any observation ever have proved the claim wrong?Science's tool: falsifiability
    VValidated. How is the claim checked? Has it survived real tests, and can others repeat them?Science's tool: replication
    CCommunity. Who counts as an expert? Is the claim scrutinised by a community that actively hunts each other's errors?Science's tool: peer review
    RReach. Is the question even answerable by this method at all — or is it beyond what evidence can settle?The one we don't yet have a clean tool for — leave it open
