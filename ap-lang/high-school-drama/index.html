The Situation

The other day, Marc Tetreault and his girlfriend of 6 months, Carly McIntosh, got in a rather large fight because he went to a bonfire at his friend Matt's house without her. (Carly and Matt don't get along, so Marc knew she wouldn't have had fun anyway). Now, they seemed to be getting along for the last 2 weeks—but since the fight a lot of information has been revealed—now Marc needs to make a decision about his relationship.

Your Task

Read the following sources carefully. Write a DM (Direct Message) that synthesizes material from at least three sources and develops your position about how Marc should handle his situation with Carly.

1
Respond with a thesis that presents a defensible position.
2
Use evidence from at least 3 sources. Indicate sources clearly.
3
Explain how the evidence supports your line of reasoning.
4
Use appropriate grammar and punctuation.

4
Your friend squad is everything. When you're single, you don't have to worry about splitting your time, or if your friends and S.O. get along.
5
It's NBD if you've never officially dated anyone. That's super common in high school and even college.
6
You always get to choose what to watch on Netflix. Compromising on a two-hour movie sucks.
7
You're saving a ton of money — no more $10 pizzas, $50 concert tickets, or constant gas money.
8
No one gets jealous when you stare at your celebrity crush for hours on end.
9
You can pick the best college for YOU without worrying about what it means for your relationship.
10
You can focus 100% on TV drama without adding IRL relationship drama to the mix.
